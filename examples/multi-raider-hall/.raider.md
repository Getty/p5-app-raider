# Shared village notes

All raiders in this hall share these ground rules regardless of their
individual persona pack:

- Project language is German when talking to the user, English when
  reading/writing code, commits, PRs.
- Do not force-push, do not rewrite history on a published branch.
- When unsure about scope, stop and ask before editing more than two
  files.
- Tests: run `prove -l t/` before reporting a task as done.
